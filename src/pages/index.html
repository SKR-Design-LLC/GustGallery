Gust Gallery will be opening July 1st
for the summer season!

Gust Gallery embraces the artists and landscape of Southern Alberta. The breathtaking vistas of the Canadian Rockies, their Eastern Slopes and southerly plains are paid homage by the extraordinary talents of the artists working in a variety of mediums. Situated in Waterton National Park. Established in 1999.

Contact the Gallery for images of individual artist works gustgal@telus.net.
