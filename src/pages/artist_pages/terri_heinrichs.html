Artist Statement

Terri Heinrichs' passion for painting is clearly evident in her art. Her Vivid Impressions series bursts with colour and lush texture using thick, creamy oil paint to abstract her unique view of the natural world.

These original oil paintings often feature the Canadian landscape, mostly local Alberta scenes from the Calgary area. Terri finds beauty and inspiration everywhere, from the expanse of the Rocky Mountains, to prairie skies, to details of wild grasses or poplar tree bark. Her work presents her love of the Canadian landscapes to the world.

Boldly abstract when seen up close, these paintings become more realistic when seen at a distance. Terri's masterful layering process blends a bright background color, subtle gold underpainting, and deftly applied oil colors to create a visual feast for the eyes.
