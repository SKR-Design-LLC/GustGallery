Artist Biography

Susan Fitzsimmons brings passion to beautiful artwork through the mediums of metal and metalworking with semi-precious gemstones. Through her artistry she puts a breath of life into her work by creating stunning fashion forward jewelry pieces while remaining authentic to her unique design and personality.

 

Susan was born and raised in Lethbridge Alberta and currently resides in Edmonton. A registered nurse for 30 years, she continues to find this aspect of her life rewarding.

 

She apprenticed under the talented and knowledge generous Robyn Cornelius who encouraged, inspired and expanded the creative process of silversmithing. New to the magical art of metalsmithing, Susan's thirst for knowledge is ongoing.

 

Susan Fitzsimmons' line Flaunt Jewelry Design exists for men and women to love and adorn themselves with wearable art.
