Artist Biography

Nicole Tremblay has been blowing glass for over twenty years. She is well known for her whimsical nature-inspired sculptures and landscapes. After graduating from the Alberta College of Art and Design in Calgary, Alberta, she spent several years living and working in Toronto and Milton, Ontario before moving back to Alberta. She currently co-owns and operates Fireweed Glass Studio in Canmore, Alberta.
