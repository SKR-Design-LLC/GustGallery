Artist Biography

Martha Henry studied glass-blowing at Sheridan College School of Design, Ontario and sculpture at the Alberta College of Art, where she taught glassblowing. She was one of the first hot glass artists to be working in Western Canada. Starting her first studio in 1979, Martha has established her reputation as one of Canada's leading glass artists and continues to work from her studio in Calgary, Alberta. Her distinctive works have been commissioned for Culinary Team Canada, Princess Diana, the Rolling Stones nad are in numerous collections around the world. Each piece is signed by the artist.
