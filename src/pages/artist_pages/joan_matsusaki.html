Ceramics

Artist Statement

Making pots for me is about creating something useful and communicating to the user what inspires me and what I find beautiful. My influences and inspirations come from many different sources - but most significantly wilderness landscapes and the animals that live in them inspire my work. I am happiest when I am immersed in nature and hopefully when someone purchases my work they are taking some of that happiness with them.

Artist Biography

I have a Fine Art Degree from the University of Guelph and a Ceramics Certificate from Sir Sanford Fleming College. I have been potting since 1999. Currently I reside in the foothills south of Bragg Creek, Alberta and work out of my studio at home.
